Getting started

Ready in a few minutes.

You only need a Telegram account and a couple of free credentials.

1
Download TeleDrive for your device

Pick the version for your phone or computer from the download buttons above. Open the installer and follow the prompts — it's just like installing any other app.

2
Get your free Telegram API credentials

Visit my.telegram.org in your browser, log in with your Telegram phone number, go to API development tools, fill in any app name, and copy the two values it gives you: App api_id and App api_hash. This takes about 2 minutes and is completely free.

These credentials are private and only stored on your device.
3
Create a .env file next to the app

In the same folder where you put TeleDrive, create a plain text file called .env and paste your credentials:

TELEGRAM_API_ID=paste your api_id here
TELEGRAM_API_HASH=paste your api_hash here
4
Open TeleDrive and log in

Launch the app. It will ask for your Telegram phone number and a verification code — the same way you log into Telegram on a new device. Once you're in, TeleDrive automatically sets up your private storage channel. You're done.

No extra accounts or sign-ups required.
