Features
01
Unlimited storage
Telegram stores your files for free with no cap. Upload documents, videos, photos, backups — as much as you want.
02
Folders & file management
Create folders, move files, rename, copy, delete. Feels just like your phone's native file manager.
03
Stream video & audio
Play videos and music directly from Telegram — no waiting to download first. Jump to any point instantly.
04
Background transfers
Upload or download multiple files at once while you use the app. Pause and resume any time.

Share with a link
Generate a link for any file. Anyone can download it — they don't need TeleDrive or Telegram.
06
Multiple accounts
Add multiple Telegram accounts and switch between them. Each gets its own separate storage space.

Photo & video gallery
Browse all your media in a clean gallery view. No digging through nested folders.
08
Fast search
Find any file in milliseconds by name, across all your folders and all your accounts.
09
Every platform
One app for Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, and iOS. Your files go with you everywhere.
